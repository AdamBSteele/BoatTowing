Tows.

There are two types of Tows, variable and quoted. Variable Tows. Variable Tows consist of a base charge and incremental charges based on the duration and distance of your tow. For particularly short tows, minimum rates may apply. Please note that we use GPS data from your Tower's phone to calculate the distance traveled on your tow. We cannot guarantee the availability or accuracy of GPS data. If we lose signal we will calculate time and distance using available data from your tow. Quoted Rates. In some cases Drift may quote you a rate at the time of your request. The quote is subject to change until the tow request is confirmed. If during your tow you change your destination, make multiple stops, or attempt to abuse the Drift Platform, we may at our discretion cancel the rate quote and charge you a variable rate based on the time and distance of your tow. Drift does not guarantee that the quoted rate will be equal to a variable rate for the same tow.
